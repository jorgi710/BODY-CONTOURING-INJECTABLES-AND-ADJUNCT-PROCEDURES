Injectable Body Contouring Fillers, Laser Light Energies, and other Minimally Invasive Treatments

Body sculpting procedures are one of the fastest growing segments of the medical aesthetic industry. Numerous procedures exist and are evolving to smooth, lift, tighten and sculpt body areas. No longer are patients only concerned with the appearance of their face, many patients of all ages are seeking ways to improve the appearance of various body part indications and need qualified providers to advise them on the best procedure for their particular issue and safely perform these procedures.

This course is the culmination of all of the aesthetic procedures that Empire Medical Training teaches and will prepare you to apply what you have learned to the next level of aesthetic care in providing body contouring procedures. The course will benefit all practitioners (novice or advanced injectors) to safely inject Non-HA dermal fillers and biologics in a number of body part areas as well as combining and using other injectable techniques such as PDO Threads to create the very best outcomes for patients.

At Empire, we are excited that our newest class will educate our clients on the latest body contouring procedures. This class will cover bio-stimulant filler injections and the appropriate use of these bio-stimulants in various body areas. Empire Medical Training has developed and designed the Advanced Body Contouring Training Course as the next step to your injectable and aesthetic training utilizing the resources of training physicians who perform these procedures within their practice.

Appropriate preparation of products such as dilute and hyper-dilute Radiesse™ (calcium hydroxylapetite) and Sculptra™ (poly-L-lactic acid) will be presented including the various protocols, guidelines, and patient indications who would benefit from these treatments including:

  • Sagging Knee Indications (Knee Lift)
  • Elbow Lift
  • Butt-lift Procedures
  • Neck Tightening Procedures
  • Upper Arm Laxity
  • Cellulite Indications (Stages I-III)
  • Other specific Body Part Areas (i.e. Buttock Lift, abdomen)

What makes Empire unique is their proprietary methods for teaching. This 1-Day Hands-Demonstration Only Training allows for attendees to observe these latest procedures on “live models” to ensuring that you will leave the course having the expertise in all of the latest non-surgical body contouring procedures and feel comfortable incorporating these procedures upon completion of the program.

The format for the course is “classical style” where there will be didactic in the morning session and early afternoon before for the “demonstration-only” practical session which will review the injectable techniques for success in treating a number of body part indications. A thorough understanding of Dermal Fillers is a pre-requisite and prior completion of the Empire Medical Training’s “Complete Dermal Filler or Advanced Dermal Filler Training”, prior experience in administering HA Dermal Fillers, or equivalency is necessary for attendance to the course.

In addition to the injectables, PDO threads for lifting and smoothing will be demonstrated as an adjunct to the filler techniques. Combination therapies for fillers, PDO threads, PRP (platelet rich plasma), and PRF (platelet rich fibrin) will be presented. Other treatments showcased during this 1-Day demonstration course includes mechanical treatments for body circumference and the latest technologies in the marketplace for body circumference, cellulite reduction, and large area body contouring. You will leave this class with a thorough understanding of a variety of body contouring modalities that you can possibly incorporate into your practice immediately.

BODY CONTOURING INJECTABLES AND ADJUNCT PROCEDURES LEARNING OBJECTIVES

Upon completion of this program, attendees will have a thorough understanding of the following concepts:

  • Acquire the necessary knowledge to identify potential patients who would substantially benefit from these therapies.
  • Discuss appropriate patient selection and learn how to set realistic expectation of treatment outcome for body contouring treatments
  • Learn appropriate preparation (dilution, combination) of injectable bio-stimulant fillers for body treatments and adjunctive treatments (combination therapies) to optimize body contouring results.
  • Gain a full understanding of how to use microcannula injections to administer bio-stimulants, including dilution schedules for hyper-dilute Radiesse™ as well as Sculptra™.
  • Understand the placement of PDO threads (monofilament and barbed/molded) for smoothing and lifting problem body areas.
  • Learn how to combine PRP or PRF (Platelet Rich Fibrin) with PDO threads to optimize body contouring treatment results for a variety of patient indications.
  • Learn how to use PDO threads, Radiesse™ and Sculptra™ with microcannula to treat cellulite including as an adjunct to conventional treatment methods.
  • Discuss the latest device technology for body contouring (radiofrequency, high frequency ultrasound (HIFU), and high intensity electromagnetic therapy). Review anatomy and pertinent anatomical considerations for most commonly requested body treatment areas as well as the pre-procedure and post procedure instructions as well as treatment protocols for optimal results.
  • Learn management techniques for complications, adverse results, and suboptimal outcomes of body contouring procedures.
  • Acquire the necessary product information, pricing and packaging strategies, and overall marketing requirements and limitations associated with the procedure. Discuss specific marketing techniques to position yourself as the leader in body contouring services within your individual market area.
